Saribel Yanez

What is a Favicon?
Its a shortcut icon used to represent a website. They are not the same as a logo of a company, but can be similar

How are favicons created?
You first have to decide what you want the favicon to look like. Then you have to find colors that work well togther and look good with the company. Then you start creating the favicon. Yiou can use photoshop or you can do it by pixels and that depends on the size of the page and how big the screen is to see the favicon. Then you upload the picture and add it to your html code.

How did I create the favicon for this site?
I used code.org and did 36 by 36 pixels. I choose to do a heart in black and white.
